Phase Two · on the line

The things that still look manual.

  • Gift ordersDedicated box-maker station at the line's head. Gift-note printed on scan — never missed again.
  • QA stopQA & Organizer merge into one role. One person owns the box before it moves.
  • SealingAuto-sealer replaces manual tape & fold. Seasonal rental — paid back in one peak week.
  • LabelingThree-tap ShipStation Mobile retired. Auto peel-and-apply labeler takes over.
  • BoxesSizes re-aligned to FedEx One Rate tiers. New SKUs require box-team approval.
  • BeddingPre-cut, known-weight. The checkweigher stops guessing; shipping math becomes exact.
